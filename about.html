About Us

Welcome to Hooyo's Idana! Where Somali and Nigerian cuisines come together in a harmonious fusion of flavors and cultures. Our story begins with a shared love for the rich and diverse culinary traditions of Somalia and Nigeria, brought to life by our team of talented chefs who are passionate about showcasing the best of both worlds.
